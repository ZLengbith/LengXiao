Privacy Policy

App: SageCanvas  |  Last updated: June 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how SageCanvas ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our Android application. SageCanvas is an offline wallpaper and color palette studio published on Google Play.

By using SageCanvas, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

1. Summary

  • We do not collect, sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.
  • We do not require an account, show ads, or use analytics or advertising SDKs.
  • The app works fully offline after installation and does not request the INTERNET permission.
  • Data you create in the app is stored locally on your device unless you choose to export a wallpaper to your gallery.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

SageCanvas does not collect or transmit:

  • Name, email address, phone number, or other contact details
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers for tracking or advertising
  • Photos, contacts, or messages from your device
  • Usage analytics sent to our servers (we operate no backend servers for this app)

3. Information Stored Locally on Your Device

To provide app functionality, SageCanvas stores the following data only on your device:

  • App preferences — such as theme mode (light, dark, or AMOLED) and onboarding completion status
  • Favorites — wallpapers you mark as favorites
  • Recent items — wallpapers you recently viewed
  • Custom color palettes — palettes you create in the Themes section
  • Generated wallpapers — metadata for wallpapers you create in the app
  • Export history — records of wallpapers you exported, including display name, date, and a reference to the gallery file location
  • Thumbnail cache — temporary preview images generated locally to speed up browsing (stored in app cache)

This data is processed locally and is not uploaded to us or any third-party service operated by us.

4. Gallery Export

When you tap Export to Gallery, SageCanvas saves a PNG image to your device through the Android MediaStore API, typically under Pictures/SageCanvas/. This action is initiated by you. The exported image remains on your device and is managed by your gallery app and Android system.

SageCanvas does not request broad storage permissions (such as READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, or MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE).

5. Android Permissions

SageCanvas declares the following permission:

  • SET_WALLPAPER — allows the app to apply a wallpaper you select to your home screen, lock screen, or both. This permission is used only when you choose to set a wallpaper.

Permissions the app does not use include, but are explicitly excluded from the release build where applicable:

  • INTERNET, ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE
  • CAMERA
  • READ/WRITE external storage and MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE
  • READ_MEDIA_* (images, video, audio)
  • QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES
  • Location, microphone, phone state, and notifications permissions

6. Third-Party Libraries

SageCanvas uses open-source Android libraries (such as Jetpack Compose, Room, DataStore, WorkManager, and Coil) to run on your device. These libraries operate locally as part of the app. We do not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, or social-login SDKs. Coil is used only to load locally cached thumbnail files, not to fetch content from the internet.

7. Device Backup

Android may back up certain app data (such as preferences and local database entries) if you have device backup enabled in your system settings. Thumbnail cache files are excluded from backup. Backup data is handled by Google or your device manufacturer according to your account settings — not by SageCanvas servers.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

You can delete app data at any time by:

  • Using Settings → Storage & Cache inside SageCanvas to clear thumbnail cache, recents, or export history records
  • Clearing app data or cache in Android Settings → Apps → SageCanvas
  • Uninstalling the app, which removes locally stored app data

Exporting a wallpaper to your gallery creates a separate file on your device. Deleting app data or uninstalling SageCanvas does not automatically delete gallery files you previously exported; you may remove those through your gallery or file manager.

9. Children's Privacy

SageCanvas is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us and we will assist with removal of any locally stored data by guiding you through the deletion steps above.

10. International Users

SageCanvas processes information only on your device. Because we do not operate servers that receive your personal data, no cross-border transfer of your data by us occurs.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Material changes may also be reflected in the app. Continued use of SageCanvas after an update means you accept the revised policy.
